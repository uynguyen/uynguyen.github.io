How billing works

Signal Hub Pro is an auto-renewable subscription priced at $4.99 USD per month. Prices in other regions are set by Apple in the equivalent local currency and are shown to you on the purchase screen before you confirm.

  • Payment is charged to your Apple Account at confirmation of purchase.
  • The subscription renews automatically each month unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • Your Apple Account is charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime in System Settings → Apple Account → Media & Purchases → Subscriptions, or at apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

Free vs. Pro

The free tier is fully functional for scanning, connecting, and manually reading or writing characteristics. Pro removes the limits on connections and workflow tabs and unlocks the full automation node palette plus replay video export. You can use Signal Hub indefinitely without ever subscribing.
