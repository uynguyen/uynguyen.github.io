Refund Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

All purchases and subscriptions for Signal Hub are processed by Apple through the App Store. Because Apple handles the transaction, refunds are also managed by Apple in accordance with their policies — we are not able to issue refunds directly.

1. How to request a refund

If you would like a refund for a Signal Hub purchase or subscription, submit a request to Apple:

  • Go to reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple Account.
  • Find the Signal Hub charge under your purchase history.
  • Select the item, choose a reason, and submit your request.

You can also request a refund from System Settings → Apple Account → Media & Purchases → Account Settings → Purchase History on your Mac. Apple reviews each request and decides whether a refund is granted based on their terms and conditions.

2. Cancelling a subscription

Cancelling Signal Hub Pro stops future charges but is not itself a refund. When you cancel, you keep Pro access until the end of the current billing period, and your subscription does not renew after that. To cancel, open System Settings → Apple Account → Media & Purchases → Subscriptions, or visit apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

3. Statutory rights

Nothing in this policy limits any refund or cancellation rights you may have under the consumer-protection laws of your country or region. Where such laws apply, they take precedence.

4. Need help?

If you experienced a problem with the App, we would genuinely like to fix it. Reach out at uynguyen.itus@gmail.com before or after contacting Apple — while we can't process the refund ourselves, we're happy to help resolve the underlying issue.
